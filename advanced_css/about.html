Our Story

Rooted in Nature

Poisonberry Apothecary was founded with a passion for herbal healing and mindful living. What started as a kitchen experiment with simple soaps has grown into a beloved Chattanooga shop focused on creating holistic, sustainable goods.

We believe in the power of nature to restore balance and bring calm to everyday life. Our products are made from organic ingredients and inspired by traditional herbal wisdom.

Our Values

  • Eco-friendly and cruelty-free
  • Locally sourced ingredients
  • Handcrafted with intention
  • Supporting community wellness
