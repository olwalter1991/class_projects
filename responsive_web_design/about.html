Our Story

Poisonberry Apothecary began as a passion project focused on herbal wellness. Inspired by traditional remedies, we blend modern techniques with ancient wisdom to create natural skincare and home goods.

Our ingredients are organic, locally sourced, and chosen with care. We believe in creating products that nurture the body and soothe the mind while honoring the environment.
