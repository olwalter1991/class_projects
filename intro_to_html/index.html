Poisonberry Apothecary

Bespoke Holistic Goods for Chattanooga

Soaps, Salves, Lotions, Elixirs & More

Poisonberry Apothecary is a small, community-focused apothecary in Chattanooga crafting handmade, plant-forward remedies and self-care goods. Every item is made in small batches with ethically sourced herbs, nourishing oils, and intentional blends tailored for wellbeing.

From hard bar soaps and soothing salves to aromatic elixirs and custom lotion formulations, Poisonberry specializes in bespoke products designed to honor traditional herbal knowledge while fitting modern lifestyles. Stop by the shop or contact us for custom orders and seasonal offerings.
