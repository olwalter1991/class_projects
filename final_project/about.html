Our Story

Founded in Chattanooga, Poisonberry Apothecary began as a small kitchen project and blossomed into a community‑focused apothecary. We value responsible sourcing, slow craft, and education.

Our Process

We prepare each item in small batches using traditional methods. We prioritize organic ingredients and transparent labeling.
